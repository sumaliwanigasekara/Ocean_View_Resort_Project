Help & Guidelines

How to use the reservation system

Login

Use your staff email and password to access the system.

Register a Guest

Go to Guests, fill the form, and click “Save Guest”.

Create a Reservation

Go to Reservations, enter Guest ID, Room ID, dates, and submit.

Generate a Bill

Go to Billing, enter Reservation ID and charges, then generate.

Reports

Use Reports to view occupancy, revenue, and booking summaries.

Logout

Use the logout button to end your session safely.
